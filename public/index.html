CAF-GPT

Email AI Agent for the Canadian Armed Forces

What is CAF-GPT?

CAF-GPT is an AI-powered email assistant that answers policy questions for CAF members. Send an email with your question and receive a researched response with references to official policy documents.

How to Use

  1. Send an email to agent@caf-gpt.com
  2. Ask your policy question in plain language
  3. Receive a researched response with policy references

Requirement: You must send emails from a @forces.gc.ca address.

Capabilities

  • Leave Policy — Annual, sick, special, parental, and other leave types
  • DOAD Policy — Defence Administrative Orders and Directives
  • QR&O Policy — Queen's Regulations and Orders
  • Feedback Notes — PACE feedback note generation

Examples

Example: Leave Question
To: agent@caf-gpt.com
Subject: Annual leave carryover

How many days of annual leave can I carry over to next year?
Example: Feedback Note Request
To: pacenote@caf-gpt.com
Subject: Feedback note for MCpl

MCpl Smith organized the squadron summer BBQ for 200 personnel. 
She coordinated with local vendors, managed a $2000 budget, and 
led a team of 10 volunteers. The event was a success with positive 
feedback from the CO.
Example: QR&O Question
To: agent@caf-gpt.com
Subject: Release process

What are the regulations around voluntary release?

Limitations

  • Only responds to @forces.gc.ca email addresses
  • Policy knowledge limited to: Leave, DOAD, QR&O
  • AI-generated responses should be verified against official sources
